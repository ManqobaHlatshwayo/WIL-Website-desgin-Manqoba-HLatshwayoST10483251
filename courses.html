Our Training Programmes

Empowering the Nation was established in 2022 with the goal of uplifting communities by providing accessible and practical training for domestic workers, gardeners, and other community members. Our courses help people gain employable skills, build confidence, and start their own small businesses.

Six-Month Courses

These in-depth courses are designed to provide professional-level training and practical experience for career advancement or entrepreneurship.

Course Name Fees (R) Purpose
First Aid 1500 To provide first aid awareness and basic life support.
